Hogwarts and its Houses!

Hogwarts have 4 houses that students are sorted into by the Sorting Hat, created by the four Founders of Hogwarts. The Houses are named after them...

Gryffindor House

Named after Godric Gryffindor, students sorted into this House exhibit qualities of brave, noble and recklessness. Their current Head of House is Professor Minerva McGonagall. Well known Gryffindors include the Golden Trio: Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and Neville Longbottom.

Ravenclaw House

Named after Rowena Ravenclaw, students sorted into this House value knowledge over everything. Their current Head of House is Professor Filius Flitwick. Well known Ravenclaws include Luna Lovegood, Cho Chang and Padma Patil.

Hufflepuff House

Named after Helena Hufflepuff, students sorted into this House value loyalty and friendship. Their current Head of House is Professor Pomona Sprout. Well known Hufflepuffs include Nymphadora Tonks and Cedric Diggory.

Slytherin House

Named after Salazar Slytherin, students sorted into this House value ambition and cunning. Their current Head of House is Professor Severus Snape. Well known Slytherins include Draco Malfoy, Pansy Parkinson, Vincent Crabbe and Gregory Goyle.
